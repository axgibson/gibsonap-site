üéØ Goal 4: AVID Enrichment Rotation

SMART Goal: By September 26, 2025, teachers will implement a rotating 3-week cycle of AVID strategies (on-demand writing, study groups, and grade checks), producing student artifacts every 3 weeks as evidence of WICOR integration and AVID-aligned instructional growth.

- Collaborate with the AVID Site Team to finalize the 3-week enrichment rotation structure and schedule.

- Provide PD to enrichment staff on the three AVID focus areas: on-demand writing, collaborative study groups, and grade reflection/checks.

- Distribute pacing guides, activity templates, and sample student artifacts for each cycle.

- Establish a shared folder in Canvas for submitting and archiving student artifacts.

- Week 1: Implement On-Demand Writing prompts aligned to core content areas.

- Week 2: Facilitate AVID study groups with clearly assigned roles (e.g., questioner, summarizer, timekeeper).

- Week 3: Conduct grade checks and student self-reflection activities using AVID templates.

- Admin walkthroughs and spot-checks to monitor fidelity and provide just-in-time coaching.

- Collect student artifacts from all three strategy weeks and review them with the AVID Site Team.

- Conduct quick surveys or written reflections from students on the impact of the AVID enrichment cycle.

- Celebrate success through staff shoutouts and student work displays.

- Adjust rotation pacing and materials for Spring rollout based on teacher and student feedback.
